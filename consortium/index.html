How immutability works

1

Private networks select smart contracts to broadcast data to Consortium Network.

2

Execution of the selected smart contract triggers delivery of the respective transaction hash to the Consortium Network.

3

Consortium Network validates the hash and stores it in the dedicated register.

4

Users may opt to either save transaction on their laptops or print it out.

5

At any time users may check hashes and contracts in Consortium Network.

6

Validated hash ensures immutability of contracts originated from private networks.
